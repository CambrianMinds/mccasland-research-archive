## Practical reading of the evidence

The safest reading as of April 16, 2026:

- McCasland is a substantially more credible candidate than the initial assessment suggested. The Harvard Kennedy School U.S.-Russia Security Program and the SAPOC executive secretary role are institutional confirmations of exactly the access envelope the `@TMBSpaceships` posts require.
- James Tegnelia's DTRA background and DBE Consulting partnership is the most significant new finding. The two men were simultaneously running WMD counter-threat infrastructure and SAP oversight machinery and are now business partners.
- Rob Weiss at Lockheed Skunk Works participating under his corporate email elevates the 2016 Podesta meeting from fringe UFO outreach to something with at least one major defense contractor treating it as a legitimate institutional engagement.
- The evidence gathered here still does not rise to identity confirmation of the TMBSpaceships account. The case remains circumstantial.
- The 38-year vs. 34-year discrepancy, previously the strongest counter-evidence, has been substantially defused by the "intentional obfuscation" framing — though this framing is itself interpretive.
- Whether the account posted after February 27, 2026 remains unresolved. TwStalker snippets suggest continued activity; the XLSX and the Sentinel's timeline both point to February 27 as the last documented posting date.
- Ashton Forbes is part of the public amplification chain, not a confirming source — but his on-camera confirmation that the schematic was sent directly to him is now a primary-source record.
