## Decision Tree: Which to Do First?

### If timeline is SHORT (< 1 month):
**→ Do Patents First**
- 2 hours of work yields concrete results
- Identifies existing prior art
- Can inform FOIA requests (target specific programs)

### If timeline is LONG (1-6 months):
**→ Do Both in Parallel**
- Start FOIA requests immediately (30-day filing)
- Execute patent searches within 2 weeks
- Integrate findings once FOIA documents arrive

### If budget is LIMITED ($0-200):
**→ Patents Only**
- All databases are free
- Minimal time investment
- Yields complete competitive intelligence

### If budget is AVAILABLE ($200-800):
**→ Both, With Fee Waivers**
- Submit FOIA requests with public interest waivers
- Conduct patent searches to inform FOIA strategy
- Appeal denials if warranted
