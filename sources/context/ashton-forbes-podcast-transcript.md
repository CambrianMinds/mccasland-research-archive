## 911 Call — New Details

The transcript includes a full transcription of Susan Wilkerson's 911 call, adding several details not in previously captured sources:

- She said she left for her doctor's appointment at **11:10 AM** (not just "around 11")
- She described him as having "anxiety, short-term memory loss, lack of sleep"
- She stated he had said **"if his brain and body keep deteriorating, he didn't want to live like that"** — but immediately characterized this as frustration, not active planning
- She noted he had "a smartwatch" and was uncertain whether it was with him
- She described leaving at 11:10 AM and returning at noon, creating a **50-minute window**

The 11:10 AM departure is notable: the last @TMBSpaceships post was at **11:28 AM MST**. If Susan left at 11:10 AM and the post was made at 11:28 AM, the account was active *after she left* and *before he disappeared*.
